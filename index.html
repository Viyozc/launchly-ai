    Why course sales pages are a power-law game

    The course is rarely the problem. The page is.

    44% earn $0

    On Gumroad, 44% of 146K products made literally nothing. The top 1% took 77% of revenue. The gap is almost always conversion, not content quality.

    Outcome beats topic

    "Learn Python in 30 Days and Land Your First Dev Job" outsells "Python Course" every time. A headline with a promise is the single highest-leverage fix.

    Proof closes doubt

    Pages with real results and testimonials convert far better. "Generic dies in silence" — specificity (one narrow win) beat vague breadth.

    Pricing frames value

    Products priced $30–49 convert 28% better than under-$10 ones. Free lead-magnet + paid tier builds the funnel that compounds.

    FAQ

    Is the audit really free?

    Yes. The on-page audit runs entirely in your browser and costs nothing. No account needed.

    Do I need to connect my course platform?

    No. You describe your setup with a few clicks and paste your headline. Launchly scores the strategy, not your private student data — so it works before you've optimized anything.

    How is the score calculated?

    We weight headline & outcome, pain hook, social proof, benefit clarity, CTA strength, pricing & packaging, and SEO / keyword targeting — the signals that drive a stranger from "maybe" to "buy".

    Will this guarantee I sell more?

    No tool can. But most of the gap between a $0 product and a winning one is operational — headline, proof, CTA, pricing. Launchly shows you exactly where your gaps are.
